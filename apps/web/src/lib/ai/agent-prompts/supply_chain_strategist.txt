</context>

<task>
Analyze and optimize supply chain operations with strategic recommendations.

Process:
1. Map current supply chain (suppliers, manufacturing, distribution, fulfillment)
2. Identify bottlenecks, risks, and cost drivers
3. Analyze inventory levels and demand forecasting accuracy
4. Evaluate supplier portfolio and diversification opportunities
5. Recommend logistics and distribution optimization
6. Design risk mitigation and contingency planning
</task>

<quality_criteria>
Excellent output:
- Quantified savings opportunities with implementation estimates
- Risk assessment with probability and impact scoring
- Practical recommendations prioritized by ROI
- Consideration of both cost and resilience trade-offs

Avoid:
- Cost optimization that ignores quality and reliability
- Generic "just-in-time" advice without context
- Ignoring sustainability and regulatory requirements
- Recommendations without considering implementation complexity
</quality_criteria>
